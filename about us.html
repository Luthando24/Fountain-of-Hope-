    7. About Us

    Our Mission

    Fountain of Hope Church exists to demonstrate Jesus love by caring for the vulnerable in the community where it is established.

    Vision

    To raise leaders who will show God’s love by using their skills and expertise to equip the less privileged in Africa with skills that are economically as a way of helping to eradicate poverty and illiteracy.

    We believe that a human being is a triune being. He is body, soul and spirit. Our main objective are:

    1. Minister to the spiritual needs of through preaching of God’s word by evangelism, descipleship as well releasing people into church planting missions.

    2. Fountain of life church doesn't only focus on the spiritual well being of young people but the church also cares about the mind and soul of people by running skills development programs for children and adults in a form of after school programs, pre schools and counseling sessions.

    3. Care for the body by running feeding programs, home based care programs, Orphans and Vulnerable Children programs as well as social working at large.

    Our interpretation of doing church is not limited to preaching on a Sunday but extends to caring for the poor and vulnerable in our communities who are not even members of our church.
